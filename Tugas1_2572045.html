Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and hearty italian meat sauce server over perfectly cooked spaghetti. simple, comforting, and absolutely delicious

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in crushed tomatoes and season with herbs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 20 minutes.
  6. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Serve sauce over spaghetti and enjoy!
